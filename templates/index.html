What We Do

We use the best in class computational drug discovery methods

Virtual Screening

Virtual Screening is a computational strategy used to identify active small molecules against a certain biological target from a large chemical library.

De novo drug design

We use our Quasar® neural engine to design novel lead compounds with desirable pharmacological and physiochemical properties.

ADME assays

The ADME assays are used to assess absorption, distribution, metabolism, and excretion. They are critical for the evaluation process of drug candidates and other pharmaceuticals.

QSAR modeling

Quantitative structure-activity relationship (QSAR) is a computational modeling method for revealing relationships between structural properties of chemical compounds and biological activities.

Exploratory data analysis

Exploratory Data Analysis refers to the critical process of performing initial investigations on data so as to discover patterns, to spot anomalies and test hypothesis with the help of summary statistics and graphical representations.

Drug-likeness prediction

We use our Quasar® neural engine to make the prediction of drug-likeness of candidate compounds, wich is of vital importance to improve the success rate of drug discovery and development.
